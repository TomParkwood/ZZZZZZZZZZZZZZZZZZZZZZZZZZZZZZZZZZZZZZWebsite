What is a Big Pad?

A Big Pad is a screen for offices and meeting rooms which will change how you conduct your meetings, with touchscreens, webcams and easy multi-tasking to make your life easier.

With screen sizes ranging from 40" to 80" so the screens can grow with the size that you need. If 80" is too small with the Big Pad Pro range you can link two screens together into one display to have almost 4 meters of screen!

With all1 screens boasting touch screen with fast and responsive Infrared (IR) 10 point touch which means that up to four users can be interacting with one screen. As well as High Definition 1080p displays2.

The PN-40TC1 was designed for Huddle spaces with the idea of improving the functionality of meetings.
It plans to do this by easily allowing for video confrencing and collaboration by using the touch screen and the easy web camera mount. Also with an optional wireless module, which allows for easy BYOD with mobile devices.
Then PEN software allows for quick highlighting and annotation during the meeting which can then be saved and sent off to the attendees
  • 40"(100.33cm) size with a resolution of 1920x1080
  • 10 point touch panel with fast and responsive PCAP capacitive touch
  • Perfect for Huddle spaces and small meeting rooms
  • Built in web camera mount no more balancing on the top of the screen
  • PEN software to allow for annotation and making notes on screen
  • Sharp Touch Viewing software give it an easy to use interface and file system to allow for better collection and organisation of data
  • With the option of a Mini OPS slot it allows for quick and easy BYOD collaboration.
  • With 1 DisplayPort, VGA and 2 HDMI connectors you can plug virtually anything you would want into it
