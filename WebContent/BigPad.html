What is a Big Pad?

A Big Pad is a screen for offices and meeting rooms which will change how you conduct your meetings, with touchscreens, webcams and easy multi-tasking to make your life easier.

With screen sizes ranging from 40" to 80" so the screens can grow with the size that you need. If 80" is too small with the Big Pad Pro range you can link two screens together into one display to have almost 4 meters of screen!

With all screens boasting touch screen with fast and responsive Infrared (IR) 101 point touch which means that up to four users can be interacting with one screen. As well as High Definition 1080p displays2.

{slider title="80\" Screens" open="false" alias="80"}

There are two different models of 80 inch screens the PN-80SC5 and the PN-80TC3A. Both offer a "Pen-On-Paper" user experience with the extremely accurate and responsive touchscreen input, which provides a natural writing. Also with the Sharp Pen software you can convert handwritten notes into digital text, as well as hand drawn shapes and lines into graphics which means that you can provide everyone with the exact copy of the notes and they don't have to make their own, so they can be more focused on what is being talked about rather than making notes.
  • Both models are 80"(203.2cm) with a resolution of 1920x1080
  • PN-80TC3A has 3x HDMI, 1x DisplayPort, 2x VGA, 1x Composite, 1x Component and a 3.5mm auxiliary jack.
  • PN-80SC5 has 3x HDMI, 1x VGA, 1x Component and a 3.5mm auxiliary jack.

What are the main differences between the two models?

The PN-80TC3 is marketed as the premium model and the flagship, whereas the PN-80SC5 is more marketed towards education offers better control over the screen with a blackout and freeze-frame button built into the screen.
The freeze-frame will pause whatever is on the screen at the time and let the controlling computer continue working, so if you want to leave a task on the screen but check you emails you can do so. The blackout button, blacks out the screen so the attention can be diverted away from the screen and to whoever is at the front.

Another one of the major differences is that with the PN-80TC3 you can pair together two screens so you can have twice the space for documents, notes and having the displays interact with each other like it was one screen.
