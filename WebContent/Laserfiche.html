Laserfiche allows companies to properly manage their documents in easy to find places and makes it easier to digitize all paper based documents.
Allows for much better searching of documents with more defined queries.
Reduce document duplication by having all of the files in one place.
OCR (Optical Character Recognition) for scanned files, get the information you need from your paper records automatically without having to enter it manually.

{slider title="What is the benefits of having Laserfiche" alias="Benefits"}

Save time and thus money on having to try and find where documents were stored.
Able to apply notes and other annotations to documents, so it allows for better workflow between different users.
Want to make parts of certain documents hidden to all but they need certain pieces of information to do their work. It offers the ability to redact text from documents so it is kept away from unwanted eyes.
Quickly get forms back, with digitally approve forms, have it automatically route to the person who needs to accept or deny, let them decide online, then have the answer routed back to the sender. All without having to print it out, wait for them to find the piece of paper fill it out then for it to make its way back to the sender.
Have task lists, lets users know what they need to do and a proper due by date, so they can see that they can't put it off any longer and that it needs to be done.

You can make some of your information accessible to the public using web portals which could save money on marketing costs.
You can automate documents to go to the right people when they need them, so it saves on waiting for documents to be sent and trying to find documents in a disorganized directory.
Like one drive or other cloud based storage options, even if the internet goes down and it cannot sync the latest version, it will save a local copy and then update as soon as its back online.
Make batch scanning much simpler with intuitive user interfaces which makes it much easier to process documents.

{slider title="How can you use Laserfiche" alias="Use"}

Laserfiche allows you to save directly to it from Microsoft Office. This also includes importing emails (with their attachments) with only a few clicks.
If you have employees who are rarely in the office but still need to upload files and get files from the center hub. They can do so with mobile devices, such as taking pictures and have them upload to the repository.
