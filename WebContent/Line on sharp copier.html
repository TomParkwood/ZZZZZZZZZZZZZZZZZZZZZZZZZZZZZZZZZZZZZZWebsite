Sometime Lines may appear on your copier.

This can be caused by a number of things.

First thing to check. Is it only on copy mode?

Then, is it only when you use the document feeder?

If this is the case then there will be a small mark on the document feeder target glass, the thin

strip of glass to the left of the main glass.

Give this a good clean and you should be back in business.

If it is on both copy and print mode, the lines are being caused by a small bit of toner stuck to the

main charge unit. Sounds complicated? It is really easy to fix this and get you nice clean prints in minutes.

Open the front door, remove the waste toner container.

You will see a green rod attached inside the front door.

Remove this and inset it into the hole next to each of the four black plastic protruding items.

Once you have inserted and removed into each of the four holes, replace the waste toner, put the green rod back into the door and

carry on printing.If you are unable to cure lines with these procedures, please log a call out and we will come and sort it for you.
