We have found a common problem with a lot of OKI Printers that use the LED print system.

Sometimes the mono print will almost disappear or become somewhat hieroglyphic.

We also see the colours either becoming offset to each other or completely vanishing.

The fix is simple.

1. TURN THE MACHINE OFF AND REMOVE THE POWER LEAD

2. Open the cover over the drums and toners.

3. Look under the open cover and you will see four black led strips.

4. Note the ribbon cable on the right going into these bars.

5. Carefully pull the ribbon cable from the led bar.

6. Reinsert the cable back into the bar, checking it is securely seated.

7 Restart the machine and test.
