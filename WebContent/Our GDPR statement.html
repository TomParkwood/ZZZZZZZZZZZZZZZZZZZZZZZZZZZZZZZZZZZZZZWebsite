General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) comes into play on the 25th May, 2018. And has been described as, “the biggest change to data protection law for a generation,”

ParkWood Copiers & Printers Data Protection

The data protection laws are aimed at stopping non-regulated businesses misusing personal information and to make sure that businesses keep private information secure. You can be assured that we at ParkWood take our commitments to GDPR extremely seriously and that ALL personal information, anything from your name, to credit card number or other financial data is securely encrypted and locked down, and will NEVER be sold to any third party business.

In the future, for any NEW contact we will be required to request consent from you to continue to send out marketing information, however; as a current client of, or point of contact for, ParkWood Copiers & Printers (we do not supply to the retail market) we will only be required to do this if we send out new information not linked to our previous dealings, but which are deemed to be considered for a legitimate use and that there is a clear prior relationship.
