Things to try before you get our help

Most print quality problems in printers are cartridge problems especially with combined toner/drum cartridges. If you can, try another cartridge or even an old one to see if it makes a difference.

Call us for advice.

We are always happy to help. Please have the printer make and model and a good description of the fault. We may be able to cure it over the phone or point you to an easy user repair before you spend your money on a needless callout.
