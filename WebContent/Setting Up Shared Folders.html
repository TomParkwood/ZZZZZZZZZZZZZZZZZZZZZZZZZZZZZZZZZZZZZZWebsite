If you want to setup scan to folder or share documents across your network you will need to setup a shared folder. This is actually easier than most people think.

When setting up scan to folder, we recommend creating a new folder on your desktop. You can do this by right clicking and then hover over new, then click folder. Give this new folder a sensible name such as Scans. However these steps can be performed on existing folders.

{slider title="Creating a folder" alias="SetupFolder" open="false"}

{/sliders}

Right click on this folder and select properties, under the tabs at the top select Sharing, then click the Share... button. In the textbox enter everyone then click Add. Under Permission Level for everyone click on it and then change it to Read/Write then click Share. It will open a screen which should say Your folder is shared, you can click Done.

{sliders title="Changing the properties on Sharing" alias="SharingProperties" open="false"}

{/sliders}

Still under the Sharing tab, click Advanced Sharing..., then there is a button which is Share this folder, select that. Then click Permissions make sure Everyone is highlighted then on the Permissions change it to Full Control. You can then click Apply and Ok on the Advanced Sharing menu.

{slider title="Changing the Advanced Sharing properties" alias="AdavancedSharing" open="false"}

{/sliders}

Back on the folder's properties in sharing there will be a Network Path:, make a note of this including the \\ because this is the folder you will enter when setting up Scan to folder.

{slider title="Displaying the Network Path" open="false" alias="FolderPath"}

{/sliders}
